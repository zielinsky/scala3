# Contributing to Dotty

Firstly, thanks for being willing to contribute to Dotty! Head on over the
[Scala 3 Contributing
Guide](https://nightly.scala-lang.org/docs/contributing/index.html), which should have all the info you're looking for.

Also, refer to our [LLM policy](LLM_POLICY.md) for rules and guidelines regarding usage of LLM-based tools in your
contributions.
